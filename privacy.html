Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

At ConvoQuest, your privacy is our highest priority. Because our app is designed to help you practice vulnerable and high-stakes conversations, we have built it with a "Local-First" privacy model.

1. Data We Do NOT Collect

We do not require you to create an account. We do not maintain user databases on our servers. Your practice history, XP, saved resumes (CVs), and app settings are stored locally on your device. If you delete the app, your data is deleted.

2. How We Process Voice & Text

To provide AI coaching, your voice input and text data must be processed by third-party APIs. This processing is strictly ephemeral (temporary) and is not used to train public AI models or identify you:

  • Apple Speech Recognition: Used to transcribe your voice to text on your device.
  • Google Cloud TTS: Used to generate the audio voice of your AI coaches.
  • Gemini API: Text transcripts of your conversations and CV data (if provided for Pro Interviews) are sent to the Gemini API exclusively to generate coaching responses and feedback.

3. Audio Recording

ConvoQuest requires microphone access to function. We do not secretly record you, and we do not store audio files on any servers. Audio is processed in real-time and immediately discarded.

4. Analytics & Subscriptions

We use Apple's native In-App Purchase infrastructure to manage subscriptions securely. We do not have access to your credit card or billing information. We may collect anonymous, aggregated usage data (such as app crash logs) to improve the app's stability.
